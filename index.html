About Me

I graduated from the University of Windsor in 2018 with my MSc., specializing in pathfinding A.I. in game development. Other avenues of focus include software engineering and web development, although I do enjoy tinkering with ROM hacking every now and then. I have worked on several software projects of varying scope, from standard desktop applications, to Discord bots, to IBM's Watson on their Bluemix platform. I enjoy working in team-related projects despite being the sole developer of most of the projects listed here, as I am experienced in agile software development and its related methodologies. I do not consider my area of expertise to be restricted to any one specific field, as each new development environment or programming language is an opportunity to learn and grow. New challenges can be simply invigorating.

Latest Projects

Feliciabot and Florabot

Multi-Purpose Fire Emblem Heroes Discord Bots

New

Introducing Feliciabot and Florabot, the ultimate assistants for any devoted Fire Emblem Discord server. These two lovely ladies come with various features for maintaining your Discord server, from looking up unit stats to searching for Fire Emblem fanart.

Use Feliciabot to display random outputs from dice rolls, or maybe you want to take a break and have some tea? She's a maid that knows all the ropes, but be sure to stay clear if she ends up tripping!

Her more serious sister, Flora, is here to aid you whenever you find yourself looking for some quick information on the hit mobile game, Fire Emblem Heroes. Use her to search for unit stats, as well as info on any skills in the game to plan out your strategies.

Unity Game - Skeleton Key

Welcome to Skeleton Key, a first-person puzzle game requiring the player to race against the clock in an attempt to escape from the house! Equipped with nothing but a dying flashlight, your job is to escape without choosing the wrong exit. Think carefully because any one of these doors could be a trap!

C# - Pathfinding Framework

I developed a C# implementation of a Pathfinding Framework uesd for demonstrating various path planning algorithms for videog game A.I. The application features several algorithms used in the industry that can be tested and compared against one another, including one I designed for completing my thesis.

Website - My Smash News

MySmashNews is the number one news site for all fans, players, and enthusiasts of Nintendo's hit fighting game, Super Smash Brothers! Read articles, watch videos, and catch up all the latest Smash Bros. news with this nifty website, complete with automated posting features for moderators!

Unity Project - Swordrun (working title)

The world was once peaceful before an evil spirit held within one of the 9 sacred swords possessed the princess of the land, turning the region's beloved queen into a sinister sorceress. The world's only hope is for the queen's sister to take up arms and shatter the cursed blade that lead her queen astray. On her mission, she encounters other brands of the sacred swords, learning to harness their unique powers in order to put a stop to the turmoil once and for all.
